Refund & Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Learn about our hassle-free return and refund process.

Last Updated: February 2026

At Trivedatatva Pharmaceuticals Private Limited, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return and refund process. Please read this policy carefully to understand the terms and conditions for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

7-Day Returns

Return within 7 days of delivery

5-7 Day Refunds

Refund processed after return received

Easy Process

Simple email-based return initiation

1. Return Window

  • You may request a return within 7 days from the date of delivery.
  • The return request must be initiated within this 7-day window. Requests made after this period will not be accepted.
  • The date of delivery is determined by the delivery confirmation from our shipping partner.

2. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The product must be unopened, unused, and in its original sealed packaging.
  • The product must be in the same condition as it was received, with all labels, tags, and seals intact.
  • The original receipt or proof of purchase (order confirmation email) must be provided.
  • The return must be initiated within the 7-day return window.

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Opened or used products: Any product whose seal has been broken or that has been partially or fully consumed.
  • Sale or discounted items: Products purchased during promotional sales or with discount codes are final sale and non-returnable.
  • Gift cards: Gift cards and store credits are non-refundable.
  • Bundled products: Items purchased as part of a bundle or combo offer must be returned as a complete set.

4. Refund Process

Once your returned product is received and inspected at our warehouse, the refund process will be initiated:

  • You will receive an email notification confirming the receipt of your return and the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned product.
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method (credit card, debit card, UPI, or bank account).
  • Please note that your bank or payment provider may take an additional 3-5 business days to reflect the refund in your account.
  • Shipping costs (if any were charged) are non-refundable.

5. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect product, we will provide a full replacement or refund:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery to report the issue.
  • Photo evidence is required: Please send clear photographs of the damaged product, the packaging, and the shipping label to support@Trivedatatva.com.
  • Our team will review the evidence and respond within 24-48 hours with a resolution.
  • If approved, we will offer a full replacement at no additional cost or a complete refund, based on your preference and product availability.
  • Return shipping for damaged or defective products will be covered by Trivedatatva Pharmaceuticals Private Limited.

6. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

1

Send an Email

Email us at support@Trivedatatva.com with the subject line "Return Request - [Your Order Number]".

2

Include Required Details

In your email, include your order number, the product(s) you wish to return, the reason for the return, and any supporting photographs (if applicable).

3

Await Confirmation

Our team will review your request and respond within 24-48 hours with return instructions and a return authorization.

4

Ship the Product

Pack the product securely in its original packaging and ship it to the address provided in the return instructions. Retain the shipping receipt as proof of dispatch.

5

Refund Processed

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund and Return Policy or need assistance with a return, please contact us:
